Mortgages

HELPING YOU EVERY STEP OF THE WAY

Applying for a mortgage can be stressful, but that’s where we come in. At LCG Wealth we aim to take the stress out of finding the right mortgages for your:

  • First home,
  • Moving home,
  • Re-mortgaging,
  • Buying-to-let.

Please Note: The Financial Conduct Authority does not regulate most Buy-to-let mortgages.

YOUR PROPERTY MAY BE REPOSED IF YOU DO NOT KEEP UP PAYMENTS ON YOUR MORTGAGE.

LCG Wealth charge a non-refundable mortgages arrangement fee of between £199 and £999 when an application is submitted to a mortgage lender for you. Your advisor will agree the arrangement fee with you before commencing any chargeable work. The actual amount payable will take account your financial circumstances, the complexity of borrowing requirements and the amount of work required to fulfil your needs.
