OUR VALUES

Protection

LCG Wealth are proud to be an Individual Representative of 2Plan Wealth Management, one of the UK’s leading providers of financial advice. This allows us to provide our clients with a personal service, whilst also benefiting from the shared resources, financial strength and protection of a major wealth management firm.

Expertise

We ensure that all advice is provided by a highly experienced Chartered Financial Planner.

Independence

We firmly believe in the advantages of independent financial advice. This allows us to help our clients achieve their goals by accessing the best financial products and services available across the entire market.

Support

As your priorities, needs, and circumstances evolve over time, we are dedicated to providing the ongoing support necessary to keep your financial goals on track. Our review service allows us to address any changes in your situation, updates in financial legislation, and enables us to adjust your financial plan accordingly. Regular reviews also give us the chance to assess the performance of your investments to ensure they remain competitive. When needed, we will recommend adjustments to your portfolio to ensure it continues to align with your objectives.

Transparency

Following your free initial consultation, we will provide a written agreement outlining any advice fees before we begin working on your behalf. Our fees are determined by the complexity and time required to deliver the advice. You have the option to have the fees paid through the investment, product provider, or alternatively, you can choose to pay them directly yourself or through your business. Please note, we do not charge fees for personal insurance advice, as we are compensated by the product provider.
